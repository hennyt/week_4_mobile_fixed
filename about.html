BIO

Hello! My name is Henny Tasker and I am a Master's student at the University of Michigan concentrating in UX Research and Design, with a secondary focus on Human-Computer Interaction. I love learning about people's passions! What makes a person excited about new technology and ways of doing things? What makes a person dread change? How do people learn online?

These experiences have left me with a desire to explore diverse online learning environments and to design computer systems for the variety of ways in which people learn.
